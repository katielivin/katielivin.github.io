The College Application Fariness Project

Making the College Application Process Fair and Accessable

The College Application Fairness Project (CAF Project) came about because a friend and I both realized that coming from a small town in the Midwest made applying to, and getting into, prestigious colleges difficult. We recognized that there was an information gap that needed to be filled. That's why we started CAF project, a comprehensive website for navigating the college process. We plan on making the website accessible to kids in our home communities and all over the Midwest.

I contrubute to CAF project by creating the logo and graphics for the website, writing content, and helping maintain the site.
