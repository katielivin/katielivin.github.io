Welcome to my humble website

Hey, I'm Katie. I'm an English major with a love for American litearture and litearary theory. I'm also an opinion editor for the Wesleayn Argus, a co-founder of CAF Project, an amature designer, and a wannabe coder (I coded this website from scratch. That's why it's so dinky). I also write fiction and short stories (so please publish me and give me money).

In short, I like making stuff. More specifically, I like making stuff that makes the world a little bit nicer to live in. This website is a place for me to show off my stuff and to write about the process of making it. I hope you'll stick around and check out my stuff.
